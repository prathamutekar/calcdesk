About CalcDesk

CalcDesk is a static, free utility website for Indian users.

Purpose

CalcDesk.in provides free, tool-first pages that solve one specific query each: salary in-hand from CTC, CTC breakup and structure, government exam eligibility by age and education, exact age in years/months/days, wedding cost estimation, and Indian traditional and metric unit conversion.

Scope

All tools run in your browser. We do not collect or store your inputs. Calculations use published rules and assumptions that we state on each page (e.g. tax slabs for a given financial year). Results are indicative; for official or legal purposes, always refer to official sources or a professional.

Brand

CalcDesk is the brand name used consistently across this site. The site is informational only and is not affiliated with any government or employer.
