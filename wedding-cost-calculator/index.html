Assumptions & Tips

This wedding cost calculator India uses typical per-guest and per-event assumptions. City tier: tier 1 (metro) applies a higher multiplier; tier 2 and tier 3 use lower base costs. Venue: budget ₹50–80k per event, mid ₹1.5–3L, premium ₹5L+ (scaled by tier). Catering is your per-plate input × guests × events. Decoration: ~15% of venue + catering. Photography: ~₹80k–2L depending on package. Attire & jewellery: fixed range (₹2–5L) as it varies a lot. Misc: gifts, invites, transport ~10% of above. Costs differ by city and season; use this as a starting point and get quotes from vendors.

Tips to save: Trim guest list, choose off-season, fix a per-category budget, and compare multiple vendors. Use our Salary In-Hand Calculator to see how much you can save monthly for the wedding.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much does a wedding cost in India?

    Costs vary widely: small weddings can be ₹5–10 lakh, mid-scale ₹15–30 lakh, and large weddings ₹50 lakh+. Use our calculator for a rough estimate.

  • What are the main wedding cost components in India?

    Venue, catering (per plate), decoration, photography, attire, jewellery, gifts, and miscellaneous. Guest count drives catering and venue size.

  • How can I reduce wedding cost in India?

    Trim guest list, choose off-season dates, book venue and vendors early, limit decoration and photography packages, and set a budget per category.
