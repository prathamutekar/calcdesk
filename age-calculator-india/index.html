How This Age Calculator Works

This age calculator India computes your exact age from your date of birth to today (or to a date you choose). It returns age in years, months, and days so you get a precise figure.

Common Indian use cases: Government exams—SSC, UPSC, RRB, IBPS and state PSC have age limits (e.g. 18–32, 21–32); use this to check your exact age for eligibility. Jobs and retirement—many employers and pension rules use age; know your exact age for forms and planning. School and college—admission and cutoff dates often need age in years, months, and days. Insurance and legal—policies and documents may require exact age. We use calendar-aware date difference; leap years are handled correctly. No data is sent to any server.
