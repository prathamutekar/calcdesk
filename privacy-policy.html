Last updated: February 2025. This policy is required for transparency and for programs such as Google AdSense.

Overview

CalcDesk (CalcDesk.in) provides free Indian utility calculators (salary in-hand, CTC breakup, exam eligibility, age, wedding cost, unit converter). We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency. This policy explains what data we do not collect, how third-party services (including advertising) may use data, and your choices.

Data We Do Not Collect

Our tools run in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit the values you enter (e.g. CTC, date of birth, guest count). All calculations happen on your device. We do not require signup or personal information to use the calculators.

Cookies & Third-Party Services

We do not use first-party cookies to track your calculator inputs. If we use analytics (e.g. to understand traffic) or advertising (e.g. Google AdSense), third-party vendors may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads, measure performance, and personalise ads based on your interests. You can control cookie preferences in your browser and opt out of personalised advertising via Google Ads Settings or Google’s advertising policy. We will update this section if we add such services.

For more information, see How Google uses data when you use our partners’ sites or apps. You can opt out of personalised ads via the links above. Non-personalised ads may still be shown based on context (e.g. page content).

Third-Party Links

Our site may link to other websites (e.g. official exam or tax sources). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read their policies.

Data Retention

We do not store your calculator inputs. Any analytics or ad-related data is held by third-party providers according to their policies.

Children & Sensitive Use

Our tools are intended for general use. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you use our tools for sensitive decisions (tax, exams, legal), always verify with official or professional sources.
